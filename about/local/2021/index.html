2021 Local DHRI Institutes run by Community Leaders

Our 2021 cohort has been hard at work, developing their local institutes at their local campuses and within their disciplinary organizations. CUNY Digital Humanities Research Institute hosted a series of Friday workshops from April to June 2022, featuring community leaders from the 2018 DHRI Cohort, to answer any questions, troubleshoot challenges, and share best practices. The 2021 cohort will reconvene virtually at the end of August 2022 to develop local institute sustainability and organize white papers follow-ups to ensure institutional memory.
